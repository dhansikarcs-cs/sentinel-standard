# Legal & Clinical Logic: Zero-Storage Edge NLP

### Lawyer Assessment (DPDP India / GDPR Europe)
The standard strictly forbids storing raw text strings or conversation transcripts. By forcing client devices to extract categorical sentiment classifications and abstract clinical labels locally before saving anything, this standard honors the **Data Minimization Principle**. No Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is created or exposed.

### Doctor Assessment
Doctors receive standardized, objective summaries of a patient's behavioral stressors (e.g., academic anxiety vectors) rather than hours of chat data. This cuts out administrative noise and flags clear clinical trends.
